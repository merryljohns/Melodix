About Melodix

Learn about our mission, our premium features, the technologies powering the sound, and the development team.

Project Overview

Melodix is a next-generation, high-performance responsive web template built specifically for rock bands, electronic DJs, and synthwave producers. Designed with a dark neon, high-contrast aesthetic, Melodix provides an immersive music listening platform directly in the browser.

Our core mission is to enable independent artists to exhibit their audio portfolios in a premium-feeling environment while keeping the implementation details completely lightweight, responsive, and cross-platform.

Website Features

Explore the premium interactive capabilities baked directly into this template.

Interactive Audio

Stream synthesized 16-bit WAV loops locally. Play tracks using the sticky bottom control player featuring timeline scrub, duration tracking, and volume adjustment.

Favorites Playlist

Bookmark your preferred audio loops. Favorites persist across sessions via client-side LocalStorage. Filters can isolate favorited tracks instantly.

Search & Artist Filters

Locate specific sounds instantly. Real-time text search matches titles and artists while responsive query buttons isolate specific performer lists.
